VERIDIAN DYNAMICS SMART HOME SOLUTION

HOME AUTOMATION

Simply put, a smart home is a property that includes one or more connected systems, such as entertainment, lighting, heating, shades or security. This is underpinned by a robust IT network and an easy means of control, allowing the user to securely manage their home from wherever they are.

HOW IT WORKS

Our Smart Hub brings all your home security controls together onto one touch-screen dashboard. The intuitive layout makes navigating between your various devices effortless.

AUTOMATION AREAS

Our Smart Home technology can be divided into three main areas which exist in a unified smart control platform:

  1. Security: Our smart home security system can makes it easy to keep an eye on your property with live security camera feeds, instant notifications and the ability to see and grant remote access to guests when you’re not at home. We also have intrusion, temperature and smoke sensors that will automatically alert you once any drastic change is detected.
  2. Comfort: Our system allows Multi-room functionality which allows you to quickly turn lights across the house on and off remotely, so you don’t need to worry about leaving the hall light on when you go to work. Additionally, personalised pre-set scenes can be configured, letting you change a room’s ambience at the push of a button
  3. Home Entertainment:Our home entertainment systems allows you to watch or listen to any type of content, when and where you want it.Our system adds multi-room capabilities to your music and TV set-up. This means that certain sources are no longer limited to a single room. This also makes it easy to stream content from a number of different sources through a single screen.From 4K screens to surround-sound and premium hi-fi systems, integrating the latest AV technology into your home will take your home entertainment to the next level.

HOW IS OUR SMART-HOME CONTROLLED?

There are a number of ways in which our smart home can be managed, each with its own unique benefits and abilities to make operating it more intuitive:

  • Touchpanel
  • Voice Recognition
  • Mobile app
  • Universal remote
  • Keypad
