VERIDIAN DYNAMICS - AGRICULTURE IOT SOLUTION

IoT IN AGRICULTURE

Veridian Dynamics has an IoT farming management solution used to increase the quality and quantity of agricultural products by monitoring the entire farm including water levels, boreholes, soil NPK and soil temperature, humidity, temperature – a farmer has a wealth of information to drive his yield while saving time and resources. All this is through connected sensors, devices and equipment. Our solutions for your farm are as follows:

IRRIGATION SYSTEM

This is an automated irrigation system that uses water more efficiently, saves time and labor costs. This system uses sensors such as Temperature sensors, soil moisture sensors, water level sensors, a DC motor, modules such as the GSM,GPRS and wi-fi modules( to send SMS’s to the user, to send sensor values) and relays to turn on and off the fan or water pump.This system can be custom built depending on the area of your land, greenhouse or no greenhouse, crops, location and source of water available. The system can also be SMS based,with an app available

RESERVOIR LEVEL MONITORING

Water level monitoring may also be used to preserve water or to study the water usage of a certain water source. We have developed an Internet of Things (IoT) based water level monitoring system.Our IoT solution measures the water level in the water reservoirs or boreholes or over head water tanks and transmits the data to a web based information panel using which continuous monitoring of the tank can be done.

DIGITAL FARMING

In IoT-based digital farming, a system is built for monitoring the crop field with the help of sensors (sunlight, humidity, temperature, soil moisture, soil NPK-Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium) and automating the irrigation system. The farmers can monitor the field conditions from anywhere. IoT-based digital farming is highly efficient, better and overall the best system.
